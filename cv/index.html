About

I work in the space between design, engineering and product — and I've never wanted to pick one. I notice the things that are easy to miss: the half-pixel nudge, the empty state, the brittle component API, the missing focus outline. I love engineered design, backed by design systems that decide the details once — so teams move faster. Seventeen years in, building interfaces still feels fun.
